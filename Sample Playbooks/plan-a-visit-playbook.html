Overview

Purpose & How It Works

Thank you for your heart to serve our community and make visiting our church a smooth and seamless process for our guests.

Purpose: To create personal and individualized engagement with a First Time Guest before they visit our church. Our goal is to ultimately create a path of engagement to help the guest take a next step. Our hope is to begin a conversation that will help answer questions and guide a first time guest through an amazing First Visit.

How It Works: Guests who may be interested in visiting our church have the opportunity to Plan their Visit before they come. They may see an ad on social media, or visit the website to check out our church and are given the opportunity to engage with us ahead of time. When a guest fills out a Plan A Visit form, their information is automatically entered into our church database and added to the Plan A Visit track. We can then begin 2-way communication with them via an app called Growth Method.

With every point of connection, we want to try to gain a response. [Response = Engagement] This will allow guests to let us know they are coming so we can prepare for them and answer any questions they may have ahead of time. They will also be able to pre-register their children into Kids Life if they choose. We encourage them to stop at our "New Here" area so that we can meet and host them.

Standards

Team Member Expectations

  • Be attentive and responsive to incoming communication throughout the week.
  • Reply to messages in a timely manner (within 24 hours or less). Communication may increase in activity as we approach the weekend.
  • Represent Christ and Life Church with integrity and life-giving communication.
  • Proactively communicate with the Guest Experience team about guests you are expecting to attend.
  • Pray (privately) over each person you are interacting with, that God would move on their hearts.
Weekend

Weekend Steps

Day of Service
  1. Be ready to receive those who planned a visit. Keep your phone near you and watch for communication alerts for anyone that might be coming.
  2. During the team huddle, make the Guest Experience team aware if you know someone confirmed with you they would be coming. The team should have a running list, so anyone on the team can host them.
  3. Guests should be greeted by a member of the Guest Experience and taken to the New Here area to fill out a connection card.
    • Team members should write "PYV" on the top of the connect card so we can identify and make sure they are connected to the correct profile.
    • When someone completes the New Here form and identifies as a First Time Guest, they will receive a First Time Guest milestone in our system which will trigger the final automated step of this process.
  4. The guest is then added to the First Time Guest track managed by Location Pastor.
Guidelines

Communication Guidelines & Tips

  • This is a designated Life Church phone number for "Plan a Visit" inquiries. Please limit calls and messages to this purpose.
  • Please avoid sharing contact information — including phone numbers, emails, or addresses — with others.
  • You are connecting with people who may be entirely new to The Life Church, so please communicate thoughtfully. Avoid using acronyms or insider terms that a person unfamiliar with our environment would not understand.

Ready-to-Send

Quick Responses

Below are a list of quick responses you can send out based on questions or interactions with PAV guests.

Beliefs

"We are a local church that is very Bible-centered, and we highly value community. Here is a web page with some of our core beliefs."

Life Groups

"We have groups that meet regularly throughout the Memphis area in different locations. Here is a link to learn more. Let me know if I can help get you connected with one?"

Kids Life

"We have a great ministry called Kids Life for babies 6 weeks through 5th graders that meets separately during the main Sunday service. They have worship, games and an age relevant message."

Axis Students Youth

"Our Youth meets Wednesday evenings from 7–9 pm at the church. It's for Middle & High School students 6–12th grade. It's a gathering that features fun, friends and great teaching from God's Word."

College / Young Adults

"For young adults, we have a monthly gathering for 18–25 year olds that we'd love for you to join."

Discovery Class

"We offer Discovery for people wanting to connect into The Life Church. In Discovery, you'll learn more about the church and how you can be part of all that God is doing here."

I Already Attend a Church

"That's great. We would really encourage you to stay plugged into your church where you're at. There is great value in being planted deep into a community where we can receive from the fullness of how God made the church a life giving community. That doesn't happen when we go from church to church.

If there's a reason why you're looking to find a new church we would be glad to connect and talk about that too?"

LGBTQ

"Hey! You can check out our vision and values and what we believe on our website."

Special Situations

Situational or Need Responses

We may get requests from time to time that are needs based. While we would love to help in every situation like this, we are unable to support all requests due to the volume we receive. So don't feel like you have to be able to answer or comply to every request. If it's something we can do great, but there are lots of other resources we can point people to: www.findhelp.org [enter zip code, and they will be set up to find the help they need] and thelifechurch.com/care

Request for a Ride

"We do not currently offer ride services to the church, however, you can always attend church online at Thelifechurch.com"
